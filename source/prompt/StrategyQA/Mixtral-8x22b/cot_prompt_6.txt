Question: Do hamsters provide food for any animals?
Solution: Hamsters are prey animals. Prey are food for predators. Thus, hamsters provide food for some animals.
Answer: The answer is yes.



Question: Could Brooke Shields succeed at University of Pennsylvania?
Solution: Brooke Shields went to Princeton University. Princeton University is about as academically rigorous as the University of Pennsylvania. Thus, Brooke Shields could also succeed at the University of Pennsylvania.
Answer: The answer is yes.



Question: Yes or no: Hydrogen's atomic number squared exceeds number of Spice Girls?

Question: Yes or no: Is it common to see frost during some college commencements?
Solution: College commencement ceremonies can happen in December, May, and June. December is in the winter, so there can be frost. Thus, there could be frost at some commencements.

Question: Yes or no: Could a llama birth twice during War in Vietnam (1945-46)?
Solution: The War in Vietnam was 6 months. The gestation period for a llama is 11 months, which is more than 6 months. Thus, a llama could not give birth twice during the War in Vietnam.
Answer: The answer is no.



Question: Yes or no: Would a pear sink in water?
Solution: The density of a pear is about 0.6 g/cm^3, which is less than water. Objects less dense than water float. Thus, a pear would float.
Answer: The answer is no.
