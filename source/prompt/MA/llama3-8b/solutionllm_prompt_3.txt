Question: ((-5 + 9 * -4 - 0) * (4 + -7 + 0 * -5)) =

Solution:
First, let's follow the order of operations (PEMDAS):
1. Evaluate the expressions inside the parentheses:
	* 9 * -4 = -36
	* 0 * -5 = 0
	* 4 + -7 = -3
	* So, the expression becomes: (-5 + -36 - 0) * (-3)
2. Evaluate the expression inside the parentheses:
	* -5 + -36 - 0 = -41
	* So, the expression becomes: (-41) * (-3)
3. Multiply -41 by -3:
	* -41 * -3 = 123

Answer: The answer is 123.

Question: ((-3 + 5 * 8 * -4) - (9 - 8 * -7 + -9)) =

Solution:
First, let's follow the order of operations (PEMDAS):

	* 5 * 8 * -4 = 5 * -32 = -160
	* 8 * -7 = -56
	* 9 - -56 + -9 = 9 + 56 - 9 = 56
