Question: ((-5 + 9 * -4 - 0) * (4 + -7 + 0 * -5)) =

Solution:
Step 1: Evaluate the expression inside the first parentheses
(-5 + 9 * -4 - 0) = (-5 + (-36) - 0) = (-5 - 36) = -41

Step 2: Evaluate the expression inside the second parentheses
(4 + -7 + 0 * -5) = (4 - 7 + 0) = (4 - 7) = -3

Step 3: Multiply the results from Step 1 and Step 2
(-41) * (-3) = 123

Answer: The answer is 123.

Question: ((-3 + 5 * 8 * -4) - (9 - 8 * -7 + -9)) =

Solution:
Step 1: Evaluate the expression inside the first parentheses
(-3 + 5 * 8 * -4) = (-3 + (-160)) = -163

Step 2: Evaluate the expression inside the second parentheses
(9 - 8 * -7 + -9) = (9 - (-56) + -9) = (9 + 56 - 9) = 56
